State by State Requirements

Can I get one RSA training course that will cover the whole of Australia?

Sorry,  each state and territory has different requirements for RSA training. Therefore there is not one course to suit all areas of Australia. Please see details of State by State requirements for the area you wish to work in.

What is required to work in the liquor Industry in Queensland (QLD)?

The Nationally Accredited Responsible Service of Alcohol online training course that we offer (SITHFAB002) is the most recent accreditation for RSA training and is accepted in Queensland.

We have a course specific for Queensland. The nationally recognised certificate issued for this course is valid in QLD, WA, SA, NT, TAS, ACT and is also valid for the Airlines.

If you need to contact the local Liquor Authority in Queensland, the contact details are:

QLD: Office of Liquor and Gaming Regulation

Phone: 13 74 68

email: olgrinfo@justice.qld.gov.au

web: www.business.qld.gov.au

What is required to work in the liquor Industry in New South Wales (NSW)?

You can now do your RSA 100% online for NSW get started now 

As a minimum you must have an "interim certificate" which you will received by completing our online course. 

The interim certificate allows you to work for up to 90 days after which time you must have a photo competency card. 

If your interim certificate expires before you have your photo competency card you will not be able to work so make sure you apply for your competency card as soon as possible. 

For more details please visit the Liquor and Gaming NSW website 

Correspondence training is no longer allowed in NSW RSA.

Click here to purchase NSW Online RSA training.

 

NSW RSA Photo Competency Card Renewals / Refresher Course

If you already hold a NSW Photo Competency Card for Responsible Service of Alcohol you must not do the RSA training again.  

Instead you must complete a $35 online refresher course via the Liquor and Gaming NSW website and they will send you an invitation and link.
